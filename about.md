# About Mason

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a passionate software engineer with a strong foundation in full-stack development, problem-solving, and building scalable applications.

## Background

I'm currently focused on developing robust, user-centric applications that solve real-world problems. My journey in software development has taken me through various domains, from game development and desktop applications to modern web technologies.

## What I Do

I specialize in:

- **Full-Stack Web Development**: Building end-to-end applications with React, Next.js, Node.js, and PostgreSQL
- **Software Architecture**: Designing clean, maintainable systems with proper separation of concerns
- **Testing & Quality**: Writing comprehensive tests to ensure reliability and prevent regressions
- **Open Source**: Contributing to and maintaining open-source projects
- **Problem Solving**: Tackling complex technical challenges with creative solutions

## Philosophy

I believe in:

1. **Writing Clean Code**: Code should be readable, maintainable, and follow established best practices
2. **Testing Thoroughly**: Good tests are an investment in reliability and developer confidence
3. **Continuous Learning**: Technology evolves rapidly; staying current is essential
4. **User-Centric Design**: Technology should serve users, not the other way around
5. **Collaboration**: Great products come from diverse teams working together
